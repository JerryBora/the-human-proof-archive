## Phase II Challenge Ideas

1. **The Entropy Calculation:** Calculate the Shannon entropy of a specific human-written text snippet vs an AI-generated one.
2. **The Sentiment Paradox:** Identify the "hidden emotion" in a poem where the words say one thing but the meter implies another.
3. **The Visual Hash:** Reverse-engineer a low-fidelity "hand-drawn" hash to its original seed.
